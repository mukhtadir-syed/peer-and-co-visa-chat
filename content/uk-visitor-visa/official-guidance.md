# UK Standard Visitor: Core official criteria

These points are distilled from official UK visitor guidance and rules.

- The visit must be for a permitted activity under the Standard Visitor route.
- The applicant must intend to leave the UK at the end of the visit.
- The applicant must be able to maintain and accommodate themselves (and dependants, if any) during the stay.
- The applicant must be able to pay for return or onward travel and other trip costs.
- Caseworkers consider the full picture, including personal circumstances, travel history, and whether the documents and explanation are consistent.
- Documents in languages other than English or Welsh generally require a full translation with translator details and confirmation of accuracy.
- Submitting documents does not automatically guarantee a successful visa outcome.
