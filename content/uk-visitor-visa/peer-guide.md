# Peer & Co Guidance Notes (advisory)

These points are practical preparation guidance and not legal determinations.

- Build one clear story across purpose, itinerary, funding, and return plans.
- Keep financial evidence consistent and explain unusual deposits clearly.
- Show practical ties to your home country (work, family, ongoing commitments, responsibilities).
- Use a clear cover letter that states: visit purpose, planned dates, funding source, and return intention.
- If using a sponsor, include a concise sponsor letter with relationship, accommodation details, and sponsor status evidence.
- Organize documents in a simple, readable order so the caseworker can follow your narrative quickly.

Tone guardrail:
- Never present this guidance as a guarantee of approval.
